Nearest Thames Clipper Pier

This app requires access to your location. Please enable location services for accurate results.

Privacy and Security Information

This web app uses your location data only to find the nearest Thames Clipper pier and display relevant timetable links.

Key points:

  • Your location is requested once and only when you click the “Find My Nearest Pier” button.
  • Your location data is not stored, shared, or sent to any external server.
  • The location information is processed locally in your browser and is used solely to find the nearest pier.

If you deny the location request, the app will not function, as it relies on your current location to provide accurate results.
